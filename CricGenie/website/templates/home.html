What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each on a field at the center of which a rectangular 22-yard pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps.

The game proceeds when a player on the fielding team known as the "bowler" throws the ball at the wicket guarded by two players on the batting side known as "batsmen".

Greatest Cricket Players

Sachin Tendulkar (India)

Widely regarded as the greatest batsman of all time, Tendulkar holds numerous cricket records.

Sir Viv Richards (West Indies)

A legendary batsman known for his aggressive style and explosive power.

Muttiah Muralitharan (Sri Lanka)

The world's leading wicket-taker in Test cricket, known for his exceptional off-spin bowling.

James Anderson (England)

English cricketer known for his prolific career as a fast bowler, particularly in Test cricket, where he has achieved numerous records and milestones.

Shane Warne (Australia)

Shane Warne, the iconic Australian cricketer, mesmerized the world with his unparalleled spin bowling skills.

Jaques Kallis (South Africa)

He is a former South African cricketer and captain who is widely regarded as one of the greatest opening batsmen in cricket history.
